General Ways to
Improve Your Searches
Only type important words
please tell me how to cook pasta -> cook pasta
Put a “-” infront of words you don’t want to include
-william sherlock -scott holmes -> sherlock holmes
Put any words your search must include in quotes
black labrador "average weight"
Use an "@" to directly search social media
womens march social media -> @womensmarch
